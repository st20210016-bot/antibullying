Bullying vs. Normal Conflict

Not every argument or disagreement is bullying. Bullying usually includes:

  • Imbalance of power โ one person has more strength, status, or influence.
  • Repetition โ it happens again and again over time.
  • Intent to harm โ it is done on purpose, not by accident.
