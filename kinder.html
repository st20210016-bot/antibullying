Creating a Kinder World

Small, everyday actions at SAIS can change our school culture.

What We Can Do at SAIS

  • Invite someone who is alone to join your group or game.
  • Use people’s names respectfully and avoid hurtful nicknames.
  • Share positive messages on social media instead of negativity.
  • Support school campaigns and assemblies against bullying.
  • Thank teachers, counsellors and staff who help keep students safe.

Project Ideas

For classes or homerooms:

  • Create posters or short videos about kindness.
  • Write anonymous “kind notes” to classmates.
  • Design a pledge wall where students sign their commitment.

Your Voice Matters

A kinder world doesn’t start with huge speeches; it starts with individual choices in classrooms, hallways and chat groups. Your voice, your actions and your courage to speak up all contribute to the kind of school SAIS becomes.

Even one person deciding to be kind, to include others and to challenge bullying can inspire others to do the same. That is how real change begins.
