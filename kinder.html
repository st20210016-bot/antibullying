Creating a Kinder World

Kindness is powerful. Every small action makes a difference.

Kindness Inclusion Community

What We Can Do at SAIS

  • Invite someone who is sitting alone to join you.
  • Use respectful language with everyone, online and offline.
  • Share positive messages instead of negative comments.
  • Join or help organise school kindness and anti-bullying campaigns.

Project Ideas

  • Create posters or short videos about kindness.
  • Write anonymous “kind notes” to classmates.
  • Design a pledge wall where students sign their commitment to be kind.

Your Voice Matters

A kinder world doesn’t start with huge speeches; it starts with everyday choices in classrooms, hallways and chat groups. Your voice, your actions and your courage to speak up all shape what SAIS feels like for everyone.

Even one person deciding to include others, stand up against bullying and spread kindness can inspire others to do the same. That is how real change begins.
