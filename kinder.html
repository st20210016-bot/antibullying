Creating a Kinder World

Kindness is powerful. Every small action makes a difference.

What We Can Do at SAIS

  • Invite someone sitting alone to join you.
  • Use respectful language with everyone.
  • Spread positive messages online.
  • Join or help organise school kindness campaigns.

Project Ideas

  • Create posters or short videos about kindness.
  • Write anonymous “kind notes” to classmates.
  • Design a pledge wall for kindness commitments.
