Welcome

The SAIS Anti-Bullying Campaign is a student-led initiative that raises awareness, supports those affected by bullying, and promotes positive behaviour across our school. This site explains what bullying is, how it affects people, and the laws that exist in the UAE to protect students.

Use the navigation above to explore each topic. Each page focuses on a different part of the story so you can research, present, or design projects for your class.

What You’ll Find Here

  • About Bullying: A clear definition and overview.
  • Impact: Emotional, social, and academic effects.
  • Types: Physical, verbal, social, cyber and emotional bullying.
  • How to Stop It: Practical steps for students and adults.
  • UAE Law: How the UAE responds to bullying and cyberbullying.
  • Kinder World: Actions we can take at SAIS every day.
Remember:

Bullying is never “just a joke”. If someone feels unsafe, scared or repeatedly targeted, it is serious and deserves support. You are not alone, and speaking up can change someone’s life.
